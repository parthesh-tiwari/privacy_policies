# Privacy Policy

**App:** ScanPDF – PDF Scanner & Tools
**Developer:** Navastra Technologies
**Effective Date:** April 25, 2025
**Contact:** parthti1000@gmail.com

---

## The Short Version

We do not collect your data. We do not store your files. We do not track you. Your documents stay on your device. That is the entire policy — everything below is just the legal detail behind that promise.

---

## 1. Who We Are

ScanPDF is developed and maintained by Navastra Technologies. If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at parthti1000@gmail.com.

---

## 2. Data We Do NOT Collect

We want to be explicit about this because most apps in our category do the opposite.

We do not collect:

- Your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
- Your phone number or device contacts
- Your location
- Your camera images or scanned documents
- The PDF files you compress, merge, split, or convert
- Your usage patterns, session data, or analytics
- Device identifiers, advertising IDs, or fingerprints
- Crash logs that contain personal information

We have no user accounts. We have no backend database storing anything about you.

---

## 3. How Your Files Are Processed

Every core function in ScanPDF — scanning, compressing, merging, splitting, rotating, locking, and converting between image formats — runs entirely on your device.

Your files never leave your phone for these operations. They are read from your storage, processed in your device's memory, and written back to your storage. At no point do they pass through our servers or any third-party server.

The only exception is PDF to Word, Excel, or PowerPoint conversion, which requires a temporary server-side process because LibreOffice cannot run on a mobile device. See Section 4 for exactly how this works.

---

## 4. PDF to Office Conversion — the Only Server Operation

When you use the PDF to Word, Excel, or PowerPoint conversion feature:

- Your PDF file is uploaded over an encrypted HTTPS connection to our conversion server
- The conversion is performed using LibreOffice on our server
- The converted file is made available for you to download
- Both the original PDF and the converted file are automatically and permanently deleted from our server within 2 hours of conversion, regardless of whether you downloaded the result
- We do not read, analyse, index, or retain any content from your files
- We do not share your files with any third party for any reason

If you are not comfortable with this, do not use the PDF to Office conversion feature. Every other feature in the app works entirely on-device.

---

## 5. Permissions We Request and Why

**Camera**
Used exclusively by the document scanner to capture pages. Camera frames are processed on-device to detect document edges and apply filters. No camera image is stored anywhere other than where you explicitly choose to save it.

**Storage / Files**
Required to read PDF and image files you choose to work with, and to save the output files to your Downloads folder or chosen location. We access only the files you explicitly select. We do not scan or index your storage.

**Photos / Media Library (iOS)**
Required on iOS to let you import images from your photo library into the scanner or converter. We access only images you explicitly choose. We do not read your entire photo library.

We do not request access to your contacts, microphone, location, calendar, or any other permission unrelated to document processing.

---

## 6. Local Storage

ScanPDF stores the following information locally on your device only:

- Your app preferences (theme mode, last-used filter, settings)
- A history of operations you have performed (filenames, operation type, timestamps, file sizes)
- Cached page thumbnails for files you have recently worked with

This data never leaves your device. You can clear it at any time by clearing the app's data in your device settings, or by deleting individual history entries within the app.

---

## 7. Third-Party SDKs

ScanPDF uses the following third-party libraries. None of them collect personal data in the context of our app.

**Google ML Kit Document Scanner**
Used for on-device document edge detection and scanning. All processing happens locally. Google's ML Kit processes data on-device and does not send your documents to Google servers. See Google's ML Kit privacy information at developers.google.com/ml-kit.

**RevenueCat**
Used to manage in-app purchases and subscription status. RevenueCat receives an anonymous purchase record to verify your subscription. It does not receive your documents or any personally identifiable information beyond what Apple or Google already provide as part of the purchase transaction. See RevenueCat's privacy policy at revenuecat.com/privacy.

**Syncfusion Flutter PDF**
A PDF processing library that runs entirely on-device. Syncfusion does not receive any data from your use of this library.

We do not use any advertising SDKs. We do not use Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, Facebook SDK, or any other behavioural tracking library. ScanPDF contains no advertisements.

---

## 8. Children's Privacy

ScanPDF is a document utility app with no social features, no user accounts, and no data collection. It is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13, and given our no-collection architecture, we do not collect information from anyone at all.

---

## 9. Data Security

Because we do not collect or store your personal data, there is no personal data of yours for us to secure or for a breach to expose.

For the PDF to Office conversion feature, files in transit are protected by TLS 1.3 encryption. Files on our conversion server are stored temporarily in an isolated environment and deleted automatically within 2 hours.

---

## 10. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data to access, correct, export, or delete. You have full control of everything ScanPDF stores because it all lives on your own device.

If you used the PDF to Office conversion feature and want to confirm your file has been deleted from our server, contact us at parthti1000@gmail.com and we will confirm deletion.

If you are in the European Union, United Kingdom, or California, your rights under GDPR, UK GDPR, and CCPA respectively are fully satisfied by our no-collection architecture — we have nothing to hand over, correct, or delete because we never had it.
